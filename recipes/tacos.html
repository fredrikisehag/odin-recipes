Tacos

Description

Delicious tacos perfect for a friday evening.

Ingredients

  • Hard taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Taco cheese
  • Taco sauce
  • Yoghurt
  • Lettace
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Slice the vegetables in smaller pieces.
  2. Fry the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Grab a hard shell and place some ground beef and taco cheese.
  4. Put the prepared taco shell in the oven for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Serve the taco shells with vegetables, taco sauce and yoghurt.
