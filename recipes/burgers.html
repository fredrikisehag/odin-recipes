Smashed Burgers

Description

If you have never tried a smashed burger before.. Buckle up and enjoy the ride!
This recipe will blow your mind when it comes to burgers.

Ingredients

  • 1 Brioche Hamburger Bread
  • 170g Ground Beef, devided by two balls
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Make two ground beef balls, 85g each. Make sure not to squeeze them too hard.. you want the air to stay in them.
  2. Preheat the pan and add a small amount of oil in it.
  3. Place the balls of beef in the pan and smash them both with a smasher.
  4. Once the burgers are starting to sweat it indicates that they need to be flipped.
  5. Add cheese and put a lid on the pan.
  6. Prepare the bread with ketchup and dressing.
  7. Serve the burgers on the bread and eat with a cold beer for best result.
