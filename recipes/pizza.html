Pizza

Description

Here is a great recipe on a margherita pizza. Great for when you want something delicious with little time.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Mozarella
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Start by preheating the oven.
  2. Stretch the pizza dough into a circle.
  3. Add tomato sauce, cheese and mozarella on your pizza dough.
  4. Put it into the oven in 7-10 minutes.
